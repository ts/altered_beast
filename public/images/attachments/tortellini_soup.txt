Tortellini Soup
Shari Soelberg

1 Tbsp. olive oil 
4 cloves of garlic-minced 
4 cups chicken broth 
1 (14 oz) can of stewed tomatoes 
1 (10 oz) pkg. cheese tortellini 
1Ú2 bunch fresh spinach 
1 Tbsp dried basil leaves
Sautee garlic in oil. Stir in tomatoes, broth and tortellini. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and season with salt and pepper. Simmer until tortellini is just tenderÑabout 5 min. Stir in spinach and basil. Simmer for 2 min. sprinkle with parmesan cheese after dishing into bowls. Serves 4-6
